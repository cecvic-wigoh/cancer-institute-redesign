# Cancer Institute (WIA) Design System

## Brand Overview
- **Organization**: Cancer Institute (WIA - Women's Indian Association)
- **Also Known As**: Adyar Cancer Institute
- **Founded**: 1954 (established 1952)
- **Location**: Adyar, Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India
- **Motto**: "Service to All"
- **Core Message**: "Trust. Hope. Cure"
- **Logo Symbol**: Kuthuvilakku (traditional South Indian oil lamp)

## Brand Essence
- **Mission**: Provide state-of-the-art cancer treatment for all patients, rich or poor
- **Vision**: Pioneers in prevention to palliation, offering ethical and state-of-the-art multi-modality treatment
- **Values**: Trust, Hope, Cure, Service, Compassion, Excellence

## Imagery Guidelines

### Photography Style
- **Patients**: Diverse, hopeful, dignified (with consent)
- **Staff**: Professional, compassionate, diverse
- **Facilities**: Modern, clean, state-of-the-art equipment
- **Research**: Scientific, innovative, cutting-edge

### Visual Elements
- **Kuthuvilakku**: Integrate subtly in design elements
- **Medical Icons**: Clean, modern healthcare iconography
- **Abstract**: Gentle curves suggesting care and healing

## Animations & Interactions

### Micro-interactions
- **Buttons**: Scale 1.02 on hover, smooth transitions (200ms)
- **Cards**: Subtle shadow lift on hover
- **Links**: Color transition, underline animation

### Scroll Animations
- **Fade up**: Elements fade in and translate Y (20px) on scroll
- **Stagger**: Sequential animation for lists/grids
- **Duration**: 600ms with ease-out timing

### Page Load
- **Hero**: Fade in with slight scale (0.98 to 1)
- **Content**: Staggered reveal from top to bottom

## Responsive Breakpoints
- **Mobile**: < 640px
- **Tablet**: 640px - 1024px
- **Desktop**: > 1024px

## Accessibility
- **Contrast Ratio**: Minimum 4.5:1 for text
- **Focus States**: Visible focus rings on all interactive elements
- **Reduced Motion**: Respect prefers-reduced-motion
- **Alt Text**: Descriptive alt text for all images
