About Cancer Institute (WIA)

The Cancer Institute (W.I.A), also known as Adyar Cancer Institute, is a public charitable voluntary not-for-profit institution dedicated to the service of cancer patients since 1954.

We are a Regional Cancer Centre recognized by the Government of India and the State Cancer Institute, the apex body for cancer care in Tamil Nadu. We offer ethical and state-of-the-art multi-modality treatment.

Founded under the leadership of Dr. Muthulakshmi Reddy, our mission is to provide state-of-the-art treatment for all patients, rich or poor.
